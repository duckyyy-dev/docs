<Card title="Initial Onboarding" icon="book" iconType="regular" horizontal="false" href="/quickstart">
  When you first join the **San Francisco Roleplay** Discord server you will be sent a DM from our bot prompting you to start the onboarding process

  Click start and begin filling out the questionare. You will be asked about your roleplaying background, how you can contribute to our server, and what plans and intentions you have within our server.\
  \
  Once submitted staff will review it within **24 hours**, if accepted you will be auto rolled. If denied you will recieve a DM and be prompted to restart the onboarding process.
</Card>

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Class 5 Driver's License" icon="car" iconType="regular" href="/development">
    To legally drive a vehicle you are required to obtain your **Class 5 Driver’s License** by completing and passing a multiple choise test scoring a minimum of 80%.After passing your **Initial Onboarding** you recieve a DM from the bot notifying you that you passed. Attached to this mesage is a button that takes you to the google form of the **CLass 5 Driver’s License**. Complete the form and submit it. Expect to wait up to **48 hours** to be notified of your result.

    A **Class 5 Driver’s License** gives you authorization to drive **cars, vans, pickup trucks, construction/utility vehicles, buses (up to 10 seats), all terrain vehicles, and tow trailers up to 4,600 kg.**

    <Danger>
      It is illegal to drive a motor vehicle without a driver’s license. If stopped by the policy you will recieve immediate penalties such as tickets, vehicle impoundment, and potential criminal charges for driving while prohibited.
    </Danger>
  </Card>
